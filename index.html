How it works:

There are five objects in different colors.
Start a round by drawing a card. The card will show two objects in two colors.
Now, be quick and click the object that is shown in its original color.
If none of the objects are shown in their original color,
click the object whose shape and color cannot be found on the card.

Example 1

The chair is shown in its original color while the book is not.

The right answer is chair.

Example 2

No item is shown in its original color.
The chair has the color of the bottle while the book has the color of the ghost.

The only item whose shape and color is not on this card is the mouse.
Mouse is the right answer.
